Why I Chose Web Development



My name is Nicholas Moss and I have a primary background out of highschool with customer service and hospitality. I found my day to day life lacking challenge and wanted to build a career in an industry that is never stagnant, so I enrolled in the University of Arizona's Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp. Since then I have fallen in love with the class and learning languages that only developers speak! I love being creative in design and having a boost of seretonin when I test code that I never knew could be done. The world of development is constantly growing and I am excited to start a career in the industry.
